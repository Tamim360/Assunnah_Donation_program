Our Activities

Distribute relief to flood victims

About 26,000 sq km of Bangladesh is flooded during the rainy season every year. During this time, especially the Banvasi people of the northern part of the country suffered greatly. The As Sunnah Foundation takes the initiative every year in the flood-affected districts of the country to take the initiative of relief activities and to stand by the distressed and helpless people.

Self-reliant project

Through the self-reliance project, the As-Sunnah Foundation provides necessary technical training and materials-support to the poor. The objective of this project is to make unemployed and unskilled men and women self-reliant by providing financial assistance and necessary materials (separately) with technical training for skill enhancement.

Iftar and Ramadan Food Distribution

The distribution of iftar and Ramadan food is one of the regular programmes of the As-Sunnah Foundation. In order to ensure that the needy siam worshippers in remote areas of the country can observe siam and worship in the month of Ramjaan, the initiative was taken to distribute iftar items among them.
